Recipe #1

These are the ingredients you will need:

  • White Glue
  • Shaving Cream
  • Saline Solution

How to Make it!

First, pour your Elmers Glue into your bowl. Second, put in any food dye. (Food dye is OPTIONAL!) Third, mix up your food dye into your glue! (If you did not choose to use food dye just skip this step.) Fourth, put your shaving cream into the bowl and mix. (DO NOT PUT IN TO MUCH SHAVING CREAM OR ELSE IT WON'T BE AS STRETCHY!) Fifth,after you have put in your shaving cream and mixed it, you will add in your saline solution. Start by adding little bits at a time and mixing until you get the feel you want. Saline solution is used to bring together your slime. If your slime is not coming together just keep adding saline solution and mixing! Then you have your slime!
