No Glue Slime

This is the No Glue Slime page! If you are running low on glue, this is the page for you! WARNING: This slime will feel more like Play Doh then real slime. It may feel more like Play Doh, but it is still really fun to play with and very stress relieving!

Recipe #1

These are the ingredients you will need:

  • Conditioner
  • Flour/cornstarch

How to Make it!

First, take your conditioner and pour some in your bowl. After that, you just add your flower and mix! Yes, it takes some time, but it is worth it in the end. This slime will feel more like Play Doh, but it is really fun to play with. Keep adding your flower and mixing until your slime/play doh comes together well. If your slime/play doh is sticky and gross just keep adding that flower and mixing. (OPTIONAL: If you would like your slime to be colorful, add food dye and mix.)
