LAUNDRY DETERGENT SLIME

Welcome to the Laundry Detergent Slime page! These recipies are really easy and fun to make because you just use ingredients that you can find at home! I hope you enjoy my Laundry Detergent Recipies!

Recipe #1

These are the ingredients you will need:

  • White Glue/Clear
  • Tide

How to Make it!

First, pour your white/clear glue into your bowl. Next, add your food dye (THIS IS OPTIONAL!) After that, add Tide into your bowl. After that mix up your ingredients. Keep adding Tide and mixing until you get the slime you enjoy best. If you use clear glue and want clear slime, I suggest using clear Tide and you will end up with easy clear slime! If you don't care about the look of your slime you could pour blue Tide into your mixture. The only difference you will get is that you will end up with blue slime.
